CFP Is Now Open

And We Are Back....With The 2nd Edition of DEFCON 91120 in 2019

DEFCON 91120, NCR Group, 0x02 meet is expected to take place on 7th July 2019.

Submissions are invited in the following categories -

  • * Technical Talks (45/30/15 minutes)
  • * Workshops (1-2 Hours)

Important Details

Info

If you have have something interesting to share with the infosec community may it be your research or your techniques, you can request for a session in our next meet by sending an email to mail@dc91120.org as below format.

Email subject: DC91120 - 0X02 - CFP - Paper title

Email Body: The email body must contain information as per the below given format:

  • *Speaker Bio:
    • Name
    • Organization/Company
    • Email-ID
    • Contact number
    • Speaker profile(max 500 words)
  • *Paper Details:
    • Title
    • Abstract
    • Presentaion Time Required(30/45/15 min sessions)
    • Category(New Research/Current Research/Open Source Project)

Speaker Benefits: DC 91120 Badge, Goodies, Slides presentaion showcasing your work and social media, Respect from the hackers community!
