#WeAreDEFCON

DEF CON NCR is a DCG by DEF CON. This community was established on 18th February 2019. Our community is open and free for all who are interested in information and application security domain. This is a non-profit organization. You learn, earn and share with the community.

Icon

Who We Are

We are a Group of Hackers, cyber security professionals, infosec enthusiasts who are residing in U.P and Delhi/NCR region.

Icon

What We Do

We perform all sorts of activities like Web application pen-testing, Mobile Hacking , Bug bounty methodology , bug hunting in legal programs approved on Hackerone or Bugcrowd, hardware hacking, and many other sorts of activities. We help each other in our community server on discord.
