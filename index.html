DEFCON 91120

#WeAreDEFCON

DEFCON 91120, NCR India, is a DCG by DEF CON. This community was established on 18th February 2019. Our community is open and free for all who are interested in information and application security domain. This is a non-profit organization. You learn, earn and share with the community.

About Us

We are a group of hackers, cyber security professionals, infosec enthusiasts who are residing in U.P and Delhi/NCR region.

Events

We organize meetups, sessions on Application Security, Bug Bounty Methodology, Network Security, and hands on Workshops on Hardware hacking and tools for app web hunting. and distribute swags to the attendees.
