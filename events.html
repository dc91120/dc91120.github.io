DEFCON 0x01 Meet Highlights

DEFCON 91120, NCR Group, 0x01 meet took place on 20th April 2019 at Eccosphere Coworking, Noida. It started with DCG panel from last year's DEF CON.

  • Followed by an amazing presentation on Left Shift Security Change in India from Avinash jain, a Security Engineer at Grofers and part-time Bug Hunter.
  • The highlight of the day was the live #AskMeAnything(or AMA) with Stok Fredrik
  • A session on IDOR and other vulnerabilties with Vikash Chaudhary, Founder of HackersEra.
  • A session on Windows Exploitation Kernel by Himanshu Khokhar
  • Minali Arora's talk on Android pentesting to get us started with tinkering android apps.
  • Closing Note with CTF discussions and walkthrough's from our CTF organizers and Prize Distribution(1 PentesterLab.com PRO Subscription to all three Winners of CTF) and everyone who attended got 'DC91120' laptop stickers.
